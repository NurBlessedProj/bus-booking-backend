# Seed Data Information

This document contains information about the seed data used in `seed.js`.

## Data Source

All data is based on real-world information about Cameroonian bus transportation systems, sourced from Perplexity AI search results dated 2024.

## Agencies Seeded

1. **Amour Mezam** - North-West region specialist (Rating: 4.2)
2. **Finexs Voyages** - Premium express service (Rating: 4.4)
3. **Touristique Express** - Long-distance northern routes (Rating: 4.0)
4. **General Express Voyages** - Western region routes (Rating: 4.0)
5. **Musango Bus Service** - South-West region specialist (Rating: 4.3)
6. **United Express** - Modern service with online booking (Rating: 4.1)
7. **Binam Voyages** - Affordable western highlands (Rating: 3.8)
8. **Moghamo Express** - Bamenda-based service (Rating: 4.0)
9. **Garanti Express** - Yaoundé-Douala/Bafoussam (Rating: 3.9)
10. **Oasis Travel** - Premium travel experience (Rating: 3.9)

- Economy: 2,500 - 4,000 XAF

- VIP: 4,000 - 6,000 XAF

### Medium Distance (200-500km)

### Long Distance (>500km)
- Economy: 9,000 - 12,000 XAF

## Bus Types & Capacities

- **Economy Coach**: 50 seats, basic amenities (AC, luggage)
- **Standard Coach**: 45 seats, moderate amenities (AC, luggage, reclining seats, sometimes charging)
- **VIP Coach**: 30 seats, premium amenities (AC, WiFi, reclining seats, charging, entertainment, luggage, toilets)

## Notes

- All prices are in XAF (Central African CFA Franc)
- Distances and durations are approximate road values
- Bus capacities vary by class (Economy: 50, Standard: 45, VIP: 30)
- Schedules are generated for 30 days in advance
- Seats availability is randomized for realistic booking scenarios
